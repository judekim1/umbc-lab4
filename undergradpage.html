Bachelor of Science in Information Systems

The Bachelor of Science in Information Systems (BSIS) is designed to give students a strong foundation in the technical and managerial aspects of Information Systems.

Program Highlights

  • Focus on both technical skills and managerial knowledge
  • Courses on databases, systems analysis, and data communication
  • Opportunities for internships and research
  • Capstone project requirement

Career Opportunities

Graduates of the BSIS program have a wide range of career opportunities, including roles such as systems analysts, database administrators, and IT consultants.
